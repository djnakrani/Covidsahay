ABOUT COVID SAHAY

Our Team Is Working Hard Day By Day For Needy People.

We Are Working As a Free Organzation For Needy People.People Can Help Each Other By This Our Initiative.And Also People Can Donate Medical Resources Like Injection,Oxygen,Medicine Etc.People Can Easily Get In Touch With People Who Need This Resources Immediately

Second Wave Of Covid Hit The People Of World Very Badly So,That We Can Have Created This Initiative For The Middleclass People As Well As Lower Class People So That They Can Get Easily Help.Now a Days People Have Everything But They Have No Medium To Connect So That There Are Many People Lost Their Life In Second Wave.

Nowadays, We Can See That Covid Is Spreading And People Are Becoming Its Victim. In Which, Many Poor Or Medium Class People Can't Afford The Expensive Medicines, Oxygen Supplies, Oxygen Beds Etc.So In Our System, Providing Free Medicines, Oxygen Supplies, Hospital Beds, Blood, Plasma Etc. From The People Who Like To Donate Or Help Others.Our System Provide Facility To Connect People With Each Other And Help Each Other.

Our Main Motto Is That, In The Current Circumstances, We Can See That People Can Not Afford Expensive Medical Resources. So Our System Allows Those Needy Person To Connect With People Who Are Interested In Helping Others So They Can Get Help Easily.
